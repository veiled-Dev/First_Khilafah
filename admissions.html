Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to ensure the best fit for your child and our school community. Here are the stages:

Stage 1

Enquiry

Fill the form and learn about our policies. We will give you our policies, assessment criteria and profile both parents and learner.

Stage 2

Assessment

Entrance examination to profile the learner and understand their current level.

Stage 3

Admission Offer

Receive your admission letter confirming your child's place at TFKS.

Stage 4

Admission Pack

Pay at least 70% of fees and receive uniforms and stationery. Orientation for both pupil and parents.

Final Stage

Resumption

Start classes and begin the exciting learning journey!

School Policies

Financial Policy

As we owe it to our dedicated staff to pay them on time for their excellent job, we need our pupils' parents to understand that:

  • Prompt payment of fees aid implementation of plans
  • You are encouraged to pay before resumption
  • The standard payment is 80% on resumption and 20% before midterm tests
  • Pupils whose parents cannot fulfill this obligation are to be kept at home. We would hate to send them back in these dangerous times, neither would we love to isolate them in school
  • All educational items are to be purchased in school. Any unbranded items will be confiscated

If you do not find these agreeable, please discuss with the school before taking any action.

Health Policy

To avoid drug mismanagement, we make it a practice not to administer drug to any sick pupil. The only exception is first aid treatment.

  • If an injury is sustained or symptoms of something is noticed in school, parents will be quickly invited to pick up the concerned pupil
  • If a child is sick, kindly allow them receive complete treatment at home before returning to school
  • Be kind enough to inform the school if a child will not be coming in that day
