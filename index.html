Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To be the school of choice for Muslim families that balances academic excellence with the quest for Jannah.

Our Mission

To provide low-cost quality education in a positive, safe and enabling environment where all learners are valued and groomed for greatness.

Why Choose The First Khilafah School?

If wholesome education, spirituality and moral development are the things you want for your child, this is the right place. Not only do we cater to their academic growth and prepare them for self-reliance, our learners are spiritual beings who care for their environment too.

Our Unique Curriculum

Ours is a uniquely designed curriculum drawn from the British EYFS, Riyadus-Soliheen and NERDC Curricula for preschoolers. The primary education is a blend of the National curriculum of Nigeria, England, Finland, Islam as well as current trend in work needs.

Quran and hadith memorisation as well all core topics have been infused such that learners do not miss out on anything in being prepared for their future endeavours of problem-solving in their immediate environment.
